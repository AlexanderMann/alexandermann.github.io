🚲🥔❄️ Fatties & Tatties

February 11th, 2023! ❄️🥔🚲

A party literally based around potatoes, and fat bikes. This is Wisco my dude.

❓ What?

  • This year we're going to have a lil' race course out on the lake!
    • ⏲️ It'll be a timed course
    • 🔁 You're welcome to ride it as many times as you like
    • 👑 We'll crown the winner with something fitting
  • BYOB
  • Some food will be prepped (dips and sauces, roast potatoes)
  • Party favours will be around
  • 🍟 We will order 5 Guys, plenty o' those delicious suspiciously vegan fries

🗺️ Where?

Home base will be out back of 1205 Jenifer in Madison WI
