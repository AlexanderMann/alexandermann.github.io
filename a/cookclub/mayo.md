## Ingredients

⬇️ _Listed in order of how you should load them into your jar for blending!_

- 1 egg
- ~8g flavour (MLT steak spice, etc.)
- ~7g salt
- 45g (white) vinager
- [the head of your immersion blender]
- 280g (vegetable) oil

## Directions

1. Load them based on what we have above.
2. Blend.
