🍕🍕🍕 Hey all! Hope this finds you well. Below is the schedule for this year's "What a Mann will do for Pizza..." bike ride. We start in Madison WI, and end many hours later in Sheboygan at the famous Il Ritrovo Pizzeria.

🚴📓 Notes (for those biking):

  • ⛑ Helmets required!
  • 👃💩 You have to bring deoderant and a change of clothes if you're cycling...that ride home would be...miserable otherwise.
  • 🔦 Please bring lights, both front and rear...we're gonna be out early AND late
  • 🗺 Route can be found here if that's your sort of thing
  • Patch kits and whatnot to keep you rolling are appreciated, but we'll all be around to make sure you don't get dropped
  • Please check back here every so often. We'll post route updates, time changes, etc.
  • This is a classic buddies and type-2 fun kinda ride: no dick-heads allowed.
